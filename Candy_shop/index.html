When did candies first appear in the United Stated?

The history of candies dates back thousands of years, originating with ancient civilizations who used honey and fruit to create sweet treats. Ancient Egyptians, Greeks, and Romans used honey to coat nuts, fruits, and flowers, making some of the earliest forms of candy. Sugarcane cultivation introduced sugar, and by the Middle Ages, candy-making evolved with spices and sugar to create medicinal lozenges and sweets for the wealthy. In the 17th century, sugar was refined further, leading to a surge in candy variety. Industrialization in the 19th century enabled mass production, making candy more affordable and widely accessible. Today, candies come in an endless array of flavors, shapes, and textures, remaining a beloved treat worldwide 1905.
